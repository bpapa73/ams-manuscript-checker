Privacy & Data Use

Your manuscript is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic's Privacy Center explicitly states: "By default, we will not use your inputs or outputs from our commercial products (e.g. Claude for Work, Anthropic API) to train our models." This guarantee applies to API usage and is distinct from consumer products such as Claude.ai, where different terms apply.

On our end, your file is processed in memory only and is never written to a database or permanent storage. Only the compliance report (not your manuscript text) is retained.
